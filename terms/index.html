Privacy policy

What we collect and why

1. Data we collect

The beta list collects email, vehicle type, country, language, submission time, and basic anti-abuse metadata. Activation and generated packages may send VIN or VIN-derived identifiers, device identifiers, package names, app versions, install profile, license status, acceptance status, timestamps, and diagnostic events. If you accept analytics, the site loads self-hosted Umami from umami.sabino.pro to collect page views and basic visit information such as referrer, browser, device, approximate region, and timestamps.

2. How we use it

We use this data to manage beta access, contact interested users, validate registered vehicles, prevent package sharing, diagnose failures, improve compatibility, protect the service, understand website demand, and answer support requests.

3. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with infrastructure providers that run Libremotor services, with the self-hosted analytics infrastructure used for consented website analytics, with a user when responding to that user's request, or when required to comply with legal obligations.

4. Retention

We keep beta interest records while the beta is active or while we may contact you about access. Activation and diagnostic records are kept as long as needed to operate, debug, secure, and audit vehicle-bound access.

5. Security

We use practical technical and operational controls to protect Libremotor services. No online service can be guaranteed perfectly secure, so do not submit information that you are not comfortable using for beta activation and support.

6. Your choices

You can choose not to join the beta list, not to activate a vehicle, not to use generated packages, or not to allow analytics. You can change analytics consent from the footer. You may request deletion of beta interest data, but vehicle-bound access records may need to remain for security, fraud prevention, audit, or service integrity.
